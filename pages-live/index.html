e-contract — Review Board

Review board for the e-contract.com.au pre-launch landing. Everything runs off the Workbench — open any captured page, review it, leave comments and raise jobs. The lists below populate from it.

This board covers the 13 logged-out landing pages a visitor sees (e.g. from your video) — Home, Register your interest, the browse hubs, sample jobs & profiles, Create-a-Job. When the full site moves over from Beta, the page list simply expands — the daily re-capture picks up whatever is live, and the contco board's email / pop-up / flow sections can be added then.
⚠️ Pre-launch behaviour: nothing on the landing can be "completed". Every finalise action — Publicly Post, Send to Contacts, Submit Quote, sign-up — is intentionally blocked and redirects to “Register your interest”. Visitors can browse and fill the forms; the only outcome is capturing interest. So review here is about wording, layout and the journey — not transactions.
