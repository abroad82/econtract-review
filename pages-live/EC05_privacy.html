Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how e-contract (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores and discloses your personal information when you visit e-contract.com.au (the “Site”) and register your interest in our platform. We handle your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Who we are

e-contract operates e-contract.com.au, a platform that connects businesses, contractors and employees in Australia. If you have any questions about this Policy or your information, contact us at info@e-contract.com.au.

2. What we collect

When you register your interest, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your business or company name (if you register as a contractor)
  • Your consent choices (for example, whether you agreed to be contacted and to this Policy)
  • Basic technical information, such as your IP address and anonymous analytics about your visit

We only collect information that is reasonably necessary for the purposes described below.

3. Why we collect it

We collect your personal information to:

  • Contact you when e-contract launches or is ready for early access
  • Send you updates about the platform and your registration
  • Set up and manage your early access
  • Improve our Site and services

We rely on your consent to contact you for these purposes.

4. How we store and protect your information

Your information is stored securely on our hosting provider’s servers, and we take reasonable steps to protect it from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. We keep your information only for as long as needed for the purposes above, or as required by law.

5. Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share it with trusted service providers who help us operate the Site (for example, web hosting, email delivery and analytics providers), and where required or authorised by law. Some of these providers may store data on servers located outside Australia.

6. Cookies and analytics

Our Site uses cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity) to understand how visitors use the Site and to improve it. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct it if it is inaccurate
  • Delete it
  • Stop contacting you (opt out)

To make any of these requests, email us at info@e-contract.com.au and we will respond within a reasonable time.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page.

9. Contact us

For any privacy questions or requests, contact us at info@e-contract.com.au.
